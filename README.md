## ğŸ” The Process-First Framework

### **5-Step Methodology:**

1. **Workflow Audit**
   - Identify high-volume, time-intensive, or quality-inconsistent processes

2. **Impact Quantification**
   - Calculate: Time costs + Financial impact + Quality costs + Opportunity costs
   - Focus on $100K+ annual impact

3. **AI Suitability Assessment** â­
   - 7 criteria: Volume, Manual Effort, Cognitive Load, Consistency, Data Availability, Error Tolerance, Pain Level
   - Score 5-7: Excellent AI candidate
   - Score 3-4: Moderate fit
   - Score 0-2: Consider alternatives

4. **Build Focused Solution**
   - Single-purpose design
   - 25-30 hour timeline
   - Standard frameworks (LangChain, FastAPI, React)
   - Real data testing from day one

5. **Measure & Iterate**
   - Week 1: Usability
   - Week 4: Adoption trajectory
   - Week 8: Scale-or-pivot decision
   - Quarterly: Enhancement opportunities
